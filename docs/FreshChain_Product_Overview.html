Financial Traceability

How the protected value is calculated

The protected-value figure is derived from stock ledger value, AI risk, AI confidence, and configured rescue economics.

05
Stock at risk

Active stock in the affected zone: 90 units priced from the stock ledger at R 5 899.1.

Expected loss

Stock value x AI risk x AI confidence = R 4 526.08.

Salvage cap

Configured rescue economics cap the recoverable value at R 4 837.26.

Protected value

The reported protected value is the lower of expected loss and salvage cap: R 4 526.08.

Platform Architecture

How SAP BTP services fit together

FreshChain is implemented as a CAP-backed Fiori suite on SAP BTP. Data, scoring, action, and proof remain in governed services rather than isolated screens.

10

CAP Node.js

OData V4 services for catalog, analytics, intelligence, admin, ingestion, and rescue actions.

SAP HANA

Stores business entities, readings, predictions, alerts, interventions, and impact proof.

SAP AI Core

Scores spoilage risk and returns confidence, shelf-life, demand, and recommendation outputs.

Event Flow

Sensor event handling and operational telemetry connect the reading to the rescue process.

Fiori / Work Zone

HTML5 apps provide role-based access for command, prediction, action, proof, and support tasks.

Data lineage

  • Store, zone, product, sensor, stock lot, and movement records provide operational context.
  • Sensor readings and AI inference requests create the decision trail.
  • Alerts, rescue actions, and intervention impacts create operational and financial proof.

Design principle

No fallback business data is used to hide platform gaps. If live data or service proof is unavailable, the missing evidence is treated as a product defect.

Operating Sequence

FreshChain operating sequence

FreshChain handles the cold-chain rescue lifecycle from exception detection through protected-value proof.

11

Process sequence

  • Detect a cold-chain exception from the store telemetry stream.
  • Persist the reading with store, zone, product, sensor, and stock context.
  • Score spoilage risk through SAP AI Core and store the decision record.
  • Build a rescue plan from affected stock, recommendation, SLA, and rescue economics.
  • Execute the store action and record workflow completion proof.
  • Calculate protected value from stock ledger, risk, confidence, and salvage cap.
  • Monitor recurring risk, ingestion quality, and intervention outcomes.

Product outcome

The closed loop gives store and head-office teams a clear record of what happened, why the action was recommended, who completed it, and how protected value was calculated.

Master data, thresholds, sensors, impact settings, and stock movements remain visible because they control the quality of the rescue decision.
