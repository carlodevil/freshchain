FreshChain

Fresh-food rescue process on SAP Business Technology Platform

FreshChain shows how cold-chain readings, stock context, SAP AI Core scoring, store action, and financial proof can be handled as one traceable process.

Collaboration: retailsolutions SA and Shoprite

Process View

What the solution demonstrates

The demo is organized around a practical operating loop. A store receives a cold-chain signal, FreshChain calculates the business risk from live system data, a user executes a rescue action, and the outcome is recorded as protected value.

Financial Traceability

How the protected value is calculated

FreshChain explains the value calculation in the same screen where the action is completed. The figure is not a slide-only number; it is derived from stock ledger value, AI risk, AI confidence, and the configured salvage cap.

05
Stock at risk

Active stock in the affected zone: 90 units priced from the stock ledger at R 5 899.1.

Expected loss

Stock value x AI risk x AI confidence = R 4 526.08 in the shown rescue scenario.

Salvage cap

Configured rescue economics cap the recoverable value at R 4 837.26.

Protected value

The reported protected value is the lower of expected loss and salvage cap: R 4 526.08.

Platform Architecture

How SAP BTP services fit together

FreshChain is implemented as a CAP-backed Fiori suite deployed to SAP BTP. The architecture keeps data, scoring, action, and proof in governed services so the demo can be explained to both business and technical audiences.

10

CAP Node.js

OData V4 services for catalog, analytics, intelligence, admin, ingestion, and live demo actions.

SAP HANA

Stores business entities, readings, predictions, alerts, interventions, and impact proof.

SAP AI Core

Scores spoilage risk and returns confidence, shelf-life, demand, and recommendation outputs.

Event Flow

Sensor event handling and operational telemetry connect the reading to the rescue process.

Fiori / Work Zone

HTML5 apps provide role-based access for command, prediction, action, proof, and support tasks.

Data lineage used in the demo

  • Store, zone, product, sensor, stock lot, and movement records provide operational context.
  • Sensor readings and AI inference requests create the decision trail.
  • Alerts, rescue actions, and intervention impacts create operational and financial proof.

Design principle

No fallback business data is used to hide platform gaps. If live data or service proof is unavailable, it should be treated as a defect to fix rather than replaced in the presentation.

Panel Narrative

Six to seven minute demonstration flow

This is the recommended talk track for a concise panel demonstration. It keeps attention on the main applications and the concrete outcome.

11

Button-by-button sequence

  • Open Work Zone and point out the KPI row: command, risk, workflow, financial value, live rescue, monitoring.
  • Open FreshChain Rescue Cockpit and press Start incident.
  • Press Live reading to persist the scenario reading and show the store, zone, and stock context.
  • Press Score risk to show the SAP AI Core decision, score, confidence, and recommendation.
  • Press Build rescue to show the planned action and the protected-value calculation.
  • Press Complete proof to record the outcome and show the protected-value evidence.
  • Open Predict and Prove briefly to show that the decision and impact are persisted outside the cockpit.

What to explain

The important message is the closed loop: reading, AI decision, rescue action, and financial proof. The panel should leave with a clear view of what happened, why the action was recommended, and how FreshChain calculated the value protected.

Keep master data to a short supporting section unless a judge asks how the calculation or thresholds are configured.
